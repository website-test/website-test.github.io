NIT Durgapur

B. Tech. Computer Science Engineering : July 2012 - May 2016 (Expected)

The National Institute of Technology Durgapur also known as NIT Durgapur or NITD, is a public engineering college located in Durgapur,   West Bengal, India. Formerly known as the Regional Engineering College, Durgapur (REC Durgapur), it is among the first 8 Regional Engineering Colleges established in India. Today it is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology in India and has been recognised as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India under the NIT Act, 2007.

B.Tech. Computer Science Engineering is a 4-year Undergraduate Course offered by the Institute.
The programme equips students with comprehensive skills on Computer Hardware, Computer Software and Software Engineering techniques, both at the conceptual and application levels.
