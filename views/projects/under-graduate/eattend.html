EAttend@BITS Pilani

Guide: Prof. Rahul Banerjee
Developed using: Java, Android SDK, OpenCV, PHP
Project type: Group

This is an Android application developed by the SDET Unit, BITS Pilani, under the guidance of Prof. Rahul Banerjee, for marking the attendance of students via face recognition.

Click here to view the app on Google Play

Role: 

1. Worked on the face recognition algorithm (using OpenCV for Java) for marking the attendance. 
2. Integrated the face recognition module with the Android application
3. Integrated the face recognition module with the PHP based backend for storing the attendance results.
